Information Regrading The Center


An incubator for nano and micro businesses is CIEC. In order to promote and support entrepreneurs, it makes use of the industry-standard manufacturing facilities/labs, 3D printing infrastructure, digital design lab, and research outputs produced by several research centers of Centurion University.
With the help of a team of dedicated volunteers, we are able to provide a wide range of services to our clients. In order to provide a curriculum in Odisha that is primarily geared toward women entrepreneurs, Centurion University has teamed up with FICCI-FLO (the women's section of the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry). By introducing them to mentors, assisting them in gaining access to money, and building connections with the market, this initiative is intended to encourage and nurture female entrepreneurs.


By providing co-working space, shared amenities centers, state-of-the-art workshops, and laboratories, CIEC has created an environment that fosters and supports students and faculty with their start-ups. Instilling a scientific mindset in its pupils through its unique educational style and infrastructure is intended to promote entrepreneurship through hands-on, experiential learning.
The numerous start-ups (headed by students) on campus are evidence of the success of this endeavor. The Government of Odisha's Start-Up initiative collaborates with CIEC to foster entrepreneurship among students and other potential nano, mini, and microbusiness owners. It supports startups at all stages as they work to become autonomous and effective businesses.
